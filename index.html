FAMOUS PERSONALITIES

MAHATMA GANDHI

SUMMARY

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar was an Indian jurist, economist, politician, and social reformer who campaigned against social discrimination against Dalits, women, and other marginalized communities in India. He was the principal architect of the Constitution of India.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar was the first law minister of India and the chairman of the drafting committee of the Indian Constitution. He was posthumously awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 1990.

WHY HE IS FAMOUS

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar is famous for his contributions to the Indian Constitution, which enshrines principles of equality, justice, and liberty for all citizens. He was a champion of the rights of marginalized communities and fought against social discrimination throughout his life.

INSPIRATION

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar's life and work continue to inspire people around the world. His philosophy of social justice, equality, and human dignity has become a guiding light for many who seek to promote social and economic development for all.

JAWAHARLAL NEHRU

SUMMARY

Jawaharlal Nehru was an Indian nationalist leader who played a key role in India's struggle for independence from British rule. He was the first Prime Minister of India and served from 1947 until his death in 1964.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Jawaharlal Nehru was a key leader of the Indian National Congress and played a crucial role in the country's struggle for independence. He was a champion of secularism, democracy, and socialism and introduced many policies and programs to promote economic and social development in India.

WHY HE IS FAMOUS

Jawaharlal Nehru is famous for his contributions to India's independence movement and his role as the country's first Prime Minister. He was a visionary leader who introduced many policies and programs to promote economic and social development in India.

INSPIRATION

Jawaharlal Nehru's life and work continue to inspire people around the world. His philosophy of secularism, democracy, and socialism has become a guiding light for many who seek to promote social and economic development for all.
